# Indian Domestic Aviation: Pricing & Market Dynamics ✈️🇮🇳

**Author:** Preetinder Singh

## 📊 Overview
This Power BI project, developed as part of the Advanced Certification in DS, ML & AI program at E&ICT Academy, IIT Guwahati, provides a comprehensive, interactive analysis of the Indian domestic aviation sector. By analyzing over 300,000 flight ticket options, this dashboard explores pricing trends, airline market share, the impact of booking timeframes, and route-specific dynamics across both Economy and Business classes.

## 📈 Dashboard Features & Visualizations

### High-Level Market Overview
* **Market KPIs:** Top-level metrics tracking total ticket options (300.2K), total participating airlines (6), and the volume/price split between Economy and Business cabin classes.
* **Market Share Distribution:** A donut chart detailing the proportion of ticket options available among the top airlines, highlighting Vistara and Air India's market dominance.
* **Airline Pricing Strategy:** A clustered bar chart comparing the average base fares for Economy and Business classes across Vistara, Air India, SpiceJet, Go First, Indigo, and AirAsia.
* **Top Flight Routes:** A horizontal bar chart ranking the busiest domestic flight corridors (e.g., Delhi to Mumbai, Delhi to Bangalore) by unique flight volume.

### Deep Dive Pricing Analysis
* **Booking Window Impact:** A dual-line and area chart illustrating how average ticket prices surge or decay in the 10 days leading up to the departure date.
* **Layovers vs. Price:** A combo chart analyzing the relationship between total flight volume, average ticket price, and the number of stops (Zero, One, Two or More).
* **Duration vs. Cost:** A detailed scatter plot mapping the correlation between total flight duration (0 to 50 hours) and the resulting ticket cost for both cabin classes.
* **Time & Location Matrix:** Matrix tables breaking down average ticket prices based on specific departure and arrival times (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Late Night) across major Indian hub cities.

## 📁 Files Included
* **`Aviation-Pricing-Dashboard.pbix`**: The fully interactive Power BI Desktop project file.
* **`Aviation-Pricing-Dashboard.pdf`**: A static, high-resolution PDF export for quick previewing on devices that do not have Power BI software installed.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Download the `.pbix` file from this repository.
2. Open the file using **Microsoft Power BI Desktop**.
3. Click on specific airlines, cabin classes, or flight routes within the visuals to cross-filter the data and uncover specific pricing insights.
